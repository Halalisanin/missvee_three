# Miss Vee Perfume — Product Copy (Concept Three)

## Product IDs: mv3-001 through mv3-005

---

## mv3-001 — Silence and Smoke (Eau de Parfum)
**Tagline:** Incense rising in an empty room. A letter left unread.

**The Story Behind the Scent:** I remember the mornings after my grandmother passed. The house was still. In her bedroom, a stick of incense had burned down to ash in a brass holder. The air was heavy with frankincense and grief — but also with a strange, quiet beauty. This fragrance is that morning. Not the sadness, but the stillness that follows.

**Scent Notes:**
- Top: Incense, Black Pepper
- Heart: Papyrus, Myrrh
- Base: Leather, Smoked Cedar

**Price:** 50ml R425 / 100ml R625

---

## mv3-002 — The Forgotten Garden (Eau de Parfum)
**Tagline:** Wild roses growing through a rusted gate. Green, wet, alive.

**The Story Behind the Scent:** There is a house at the end of our street that no one has lived in for years. The garden has taken over. One spring, the fence collapsed, and I walked in. The roses had grown wild — tangled, unpruned, more beautiful than any bouquet. This scent is that garden: not curated, not controlled. Growing anyway.

**Scent Notes:**
- Top: Bergamot, Nettle
- Heart: Rose Absolute, Violet Leaf
- Base: White Musk, Wet Earth

**Price:** 50ml R425 / 100ml R625

## mv3-003 — Letters to Nobody (Eau de Parfum)
**Tagline:** Paper, ink, and the scent of words never sent.

**The Story Behind the Scent:** I used to write letters I never mailed. To people I had lost, to versions of myself, to places I missed. This fragrance smells like that drawer full of unsent letters. The paper has aged. The ink has faded. But the truth of what was written remains.

**Scent Notes:**
- Top: Lime, Pink Pepper
- Heart: Iris, Paper
- Base: Sandalwood, Vanilla

**Price:** 50ml R425 / 100ml R625

## mv3-004 — Ember and Ash (Reed Diffuser)
**Tagline:** The warmth of smoke long after the fire has gone.

**The Story Behind the Scent:** The best part of a fire is not the flame — it is the hour after, when the embers glow orange and the air smells of wood and quiet. This diffuser brings that warmth into your home. It does not shout. It settles into the corners of a room like a memory you cannot quite place.

**Scent Notes:**
- Top: Smoked Tea, Clove
- Heart: Charcoal, Dried Tobacco
- Base: Amber, Soft Leather

**Price:** R325 (200ml)

---

## mv3-005 — Rain on Tin Roof (Reed Diffuser)
**Tagline:** The first storm of summer. Clean. Fresh. Alive.

**The Story Behind the Scent:** Growing up in Ekurhuleni, summer meant one thing: the first rain on a tin roof. That sound. That smell. Dust meeting water. Concrete cooling. The world rinsed clean. I wanted to bottle that exact moment — not "rain" in general, but the specific smell of the first rain after a long dry spell.

**Scent Notes:**
- Top: Wet Concrete, Lime
- Heart: Linen, White Flowers
- Base: Clear Musk, Soft Woods

**Price:** R325 (200ml)

The Story Behind the Scent

I remember the mornings after my grandmother passed. The house was still. In her bedroom, a stick of incense had burned down to ash in a brass holder. The air was heavy with frankincense and grief — but also with a strange, quiet beauty. This fragrance is that morning. Not the sadness, but the stillness that follows.

Silence and Smoke opens with a crack of black pepper and the dry, sacred warmth of frankincense. At its heart, papyrus and myrrh weave a texture like old parchment — something written and sealed. The base settles into smoked cedar and leather: a scent that is both ancient and intimately present.

For those who find beauty in stillness. For the quiet ones who carry depth.

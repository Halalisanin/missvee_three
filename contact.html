Questions Often Asked

Are your fragrances natural?
Every Miss Vee fragrance blends natural essential oils with high-quality fragrance oils. I believe a beautiful scent does not have to pick between natural and synthetic. It just needs to be well made. All products are vegan and cruelty-free.
How long does a bottle last?
Our 50ml Eau de Parfum lasts about 3 to 4 months with daily use of 2 to 3 sprays. The 100ml size gives you 6 to 8 months. Keep your perfume in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight to preserve it.
Do you offer samples?
I do. A 2ml sample of any fragrance is R25. Samples are the best way to find out if a scent speaks to you. I recommend living with a sample for at least a week before deciding on a full bottle.
Can I return a fragrance if I do not like it?
Yes. If a scent does not feel like yours, return it within 14 days of delivery. The product must be unused and in its original packaging. I will process a full refund. I cannot accept returns on samples.
Do you ship internationally?
Right now I ship within South Africa only. International shipping is something I am working toward. If you are outside SA and would like to know when this changes, subscribe to the newsletter. I will share the news there first.
